About.

Because a soft touch can mean a lot.

We're a company that thinks even something basic, like a box of tissues, can really matter to people. From how our tissues feel on your skin to how our packaging looks in your space, we put a lot into making them comfy, clean, and good for the environment.

We want to make really good tissues that make life easier and cleaner at Tissue Box, work, and everywhere else. We're all about doing things well, being responsible, and coming up with new ideas – always focusing on quality and care.

🌿 Our Story

We started with a simple idea: make everyday stuff better. We noticed that tissues – small, cheap, and easily forgotten – are actually important. Whether you're wiping a kid's face, cleaning your hands, or just need comfort when you're sick, a tissue is about being clean, caring, and showing you care.

We started small, but now we make all kinds of tissues for different situations. Over time, we've put money into new tech, earth-friendly ways of doing things, and cool designs to make something that's helpful.

Now, you can find our tissues in Tissue Boxs, offices, hotels, hospitals, schools – basically, anywhere you need to be clean. We're quietly making things a little better, one tissue at a time.

🎯 Our Goal

We want to change how people think about everyday cleanliness with tissues that are reliable, earth-friendly, and well-made.
We believe being clean shouldn't hurt the planet, and being comfy should always be possible.

By always trying to improve our tech, materials, and packaging, we want our tissues to be the best they can be – safe, earth-friendly, and still affordable.

🏭 What We Make

We have all kinds of tissues for different uses:

Facial Tissues: Super soft, perfect for taking care of your skin.

Napkins: Good at soaking up spills, great for eating.

All-Purpose Tissues: Useful for cleaning at Tissue Box or in the office.

Tissues for Cars and Travel: Small and easy to bring with you.

Boxes That Look Good and Can Be Refilled: Nice-looking boxes that you can use again.

Each box is easy to open, and the tissues come out one at a time to keep things clean. The boxes also look good!

🔬 How We Make Them

We make our tissues in places that use modern machines, keep things very clean, and care about the environment.

Each tissue goes through a careful process:

Getting the Pulp Ready: We get pulp from good sources – trees, recycled paper, or bamboo.

Making the Sheets: The pulp turns into thin, soft, strong sheets of tissue.

Drying and Making Them Feel Good: We dry the sheets and give them a special pattern to help them soak up more stuff and feel nice.

Cutting and Folding: Machines make sure they're all the same size and fold them so they come out of the box easily.

Checking the Quality: We test each batch for softness, how well they soak up stuff, strength, and cleanliness.

Packaging: We put the tissues in boxes made of stuff that can be recycled to keep them safe from dust and water.

Our factories follow rules to make sure everything is safe and good quality.

🌍 Taking Care of the Planet

We know making tissues uses natural resources, so we take that seriously.
We focus on three main things:

1. Earth-Friendly Materials

We get our pulp from places that take care of forests, and we use more bamboo and recycled paper. This means using fewer trees and helps reuse materials.

2. Making Things Cleanly

We try to use as little water and energy as possible. We have machines and systems to clean wastewater so we don't hurt the environment.
We also keep an eye on air pollution, waste, and recycling.

3. Packaging That's Good for the Earth

Our tissue boxes are mostly made of cardboard that can be recycled. We're also working on coatings and refills that don't use plastic to cut down on waste. We tell people to reuse the nice-looking boxes to avoid using too much packaging.

We want a world where being comfy and clean doesn't hurt the earth. We want every tissue to be a little bit gentle.

💡 New Ideas and Designs

Coming up with new ideas is a big part of what we do. From the materials we use to how we design the boxes, we're always looking for ways to make things more comfy, clean, and nice to look at.

Some examples:

Better Patterns: Making tissues softer and able to soak up more without using extra material.

Tissues with Lotion: Adding things like aloe vera to make them gentle on your skin.

Small and Smart Designs: Making tissues that are easy to use in cars or small spaces.

Refills: Letting people reuse the boxes to be more earth-friendly.

We think design is about more than just how things look. It's about how easy things are to use and how good they are for the planet.
