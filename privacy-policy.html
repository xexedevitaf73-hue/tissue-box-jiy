​Privacy Policy

*1. What Info We Grab**

We collect a few kinds of info:

**a. Stuff You Tell Us**

*   **Account Info:** Like your name, email, phone number, password, and where you get your mail.

*   **Payment Stuff:** Credit card numbers and billing info. Don't worry, this is handled securely by other company.

*   **What you say:** Messages you send us, questions, feedback, or any other emails.

*   **Extra Stuff (Optional):** Things like your age range, survey answers, and what you're into.

**b. Info We Snag Automatically**

*   **Device Info:** Your IP address, what browser you're using, what kind of computer you have, and device IDs.

*   **How You Use Our Site:** What pages you visit, how long you stay, where you came from, what you click on, and how you get around the site.

*   **Cookies:** Little files that sit on your device to make things work better and see how you use our site.

**c. Info From Other People**

We might get stuff from:

*   Social media or login buddies (like Google, Facebook, or Apple).
*   Payment companies, data companies, and marketing peeps.
*   Public records or our partners.

**2. How We Put Your Info to Work**

We might use your personal info to:

*   Keep our services up, running, and working.
*   Take payments and make sure deals go through.
*   Handle your account and stay in touch.
*   Send you news, special deals, and newsletters (if you're cool with it).
*   Make our website better and keep you happy.
*   Spot and stop fraudsters or anyone messing around where they shouldn't be.
*   Follow the rules and laws.
*   Do research and keep track of things internally.

We only mess with your info when we have a good reason, like you saying it's okay, needing it for a deal, or if it's for our business.

**3. Who We Share Info With**

We don't sell your personal details. We only share it if:

*   **Service People:** Companies that process payments, handle IT, host our site, look at data, or send emails.
*   **Our Buddies:** For internal business stuff.
*   **The Law:** If we're told to by law or some legal thing.
*   **If the Company Changes Hands:** If we get bought out or sell stuff off.
*   **If You Say It's Okay:** If you give us the thumbs up.

Everyone we share with has to keep things secret and protect your info.

**4. Cookies**

We use cookies to:

*   Remember what you like and how you set things up.
*   Make everything work better and faster.
*   See how people are using our site.
*   Show you ads that might interest you (if allowed).

You can turn off cookies in your browser, but some things might not work right if you do.

**5. How Long We Keep Your Stuff**

We only keep your info as long as we need it to do what we said we would in this policy, or if the law says we have to. When we don't need it anymore, we delete it or scramble it so no one knows it's you.

**6. Keeping Your Data Safe**

We use different methods to protect your data:

*   Encrypting confidential information.
*   Storing and transferring data securely.
*   Limiting who can get to it.
*   Checking our security regularly.

While we do our best to protect your info, nothing on the internet is 100% safe.

**7. Your Rights**

Depending on where you live, you might have the right to:

*   See your personal info and get a copy.
*   Fix stuff that's wrong.
*   Ask us to get rid of your personal details.
*   Tell us to stop using your info.
*   Take back your permission.
*   Get your info in a way you can take somewhere else.

To use these rights, email us. We might need to check who you are before we do anything.

**8. Where Your Info Ends Up**

Your info might end up in countries that aren't your own. If needed, we protect it by:

*   Using standard contract clauses.
*   Following the rules of certain authorities.
*   Using company-wide rules or similar protections.

**9. Kids' Privacy**

We don't knowingly grab info from kids under 13. If we realize we've gotten a kid's info, we'll delete it ASAP.

**10. Links to Other Sites**

Our services might have links to other websites. We aren't in charge of their privacy or what they post. Read their privacy policies.

**11. Policy Updates**

We might update this policy now and then to keep up with legal, techie, or business changes. Updates will be posted on this page and we might tell you directly if it's a huge change.
